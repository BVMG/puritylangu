Hillary “Kidlarry”

Hillary Simiyu, better known as Kidlary, is absolutely killing it in the gospel hip hop game! This dude’s a triple threat—rapping, singing, and songwriting with a style that’s all his own. He’s out here weaving different genres together, dropping hard-hitting bars, soulful hooks, and lyrics that tell real, raw stories straight from the heart. Everything he creates is anchored in faith, packed with messages of hope, grit, and purpose that lift you up and keep you grounded. Kidlary’s not just sticking to one sound—he’s pushing boundaries, mixing it up, and still keeping it true to his gospel roots. Every verse and melody feels like a call to action, a ministry in motion, reaching people from all corners with passion and authenticity.

Mavoo

Mavoo is a passionate gospel artist known for his unique ministry on the streets of Nairobi and in public transport, where he shares the message of hope through a simple Bluetooth speaker. His music is more than entertainment—it is a lifeline of encouragement and faith for many who listen. Beyond his artistry, Mavoo has devoted himself to mentoring and counseling street boys, walking with them through their struggles and guiding them toward a brighter future. Through faith, encouragement, and genuine care, he has transformed lives, turning pain into purpose and hopelessness into hope.

Kipngetich Brian

Kipngetich Brayoo is a gospel artist passionate about spreading the message of hope and faith through music. His ministry is more than melodies—it is a calling to uplift hearts and inspire lives with the truth of God’s word. Beyond his music, Brayoo serves as a host, creating a platform where pastors and fellow artists can share their journeys, testimonies, and inspirations. Through these meaningful conversations, he reaches wider audiences, giving voice to stories that encourage faith, resilience, and purpose.
